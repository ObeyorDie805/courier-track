Party Bus & Limo Tracker

Real‑time luxury ride tracking and route updates for your party bus or limo

Driver App Passenger Page

Key Features

Live Ride Tracking

Chauffeurs broadcast the real‑time location of your party bus or limo using the browser’s geolocation API, so your guests always know where the ride is.

VIP Share Links

Create a private, shareable URL for your special event so guests can track the ride without downloading any apps.

Route & Stop Changes

Need to pick up another friend or change venues? Guests can submit new destinations, and your chauffeur sees the updates instantly.

Luxury Experience

A polished, mobile‑friendly design ensures the technology matches the upscale experience of your limo or party bus service.

How It Works

  1. Chauffeurs start a ride on the driver app when your party bus or limo heads out.
  2. The vehicle’s location is captured using the Geolocation API and displayed on a live map.
  3. A private link is generated so guests can view the ride’s progress on the passenger page.
  4. Guests can request additional stops or changes in destination, and the chauffeur sees these updates instantly.
